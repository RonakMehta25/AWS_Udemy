SNS

Simple Notfication Service. Push service.

Push notification to google,apple,fire OS and windows as well as android device in china with Baidu Cloud Push. SMS,email,http endpoint

SNS is also used to trigger lambda function.

SNS messages are stored redundently across multiple availability zones.

It is inexpensive , pay as you go service.
